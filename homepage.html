Search the Collections

Fleet Search is a discovery tool: it enables searching across many resources at once. It includes results from the library catalog and most of our databases, as well as materials you can request from other libraries.
Fleet Library shares its library catalog with the Providence Athenaeum. Look for a "RISD" or "ATH" location to determine where each item can be found.
Search across this website's pages and research guides for information on using library resources and services.
